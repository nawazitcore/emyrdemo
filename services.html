Green Building Certifications

Expert guidance through globally recognized rating systems

🌍 LEED — Leadership in Energy & Environmental Design

The most widely used green building rating system in the world, developed by the U.S. Green Building Council. LEED provides a framework for healthy, efficient, carbon and cost-saving green buildings.

✓ Global sustainability certification
✓ Applicable to all building types
✓ Multiple certification levels
✓ Credit optimization strategy
✓ Complete documentation support
✓ Review management

🇮🇳 IGBC — Indian Green Building Council

India-focused green building rating systems developed by CII. IGBC offers customized rating tools for new buildings, existing buildings, factories, townships, SEZs, and more.

✓ India-specific rating systems
✓ New & existing buildings
✓ Green homes certification
✓ Factory & township ratings
✓ Healthcare & school facilities
✓ Net zero certification

🏛️ GRIHA — Green Rating for Integrated Habitat Assessment

India's national green building rating system, endorsed by the Government of India. GRIHA evaluates the environmental performance of a building holistically over its entire life cycle.

✓ National green building standard
✓ Government-endorsed rating
✓ Life cycle assessment focus
✓ Site planning optimization
✓ Indoor environment quality
✓ Innovation credits
