About Us

Building a greener future through expert consultancy and innovation

Who We Are

EMYR Green Building Consultants

EMYR Green Building Consultants is a leading sustainability consultancy dedicated to transforming the built environment. We help developers, architects, and organizations create energy-efficient, environmentally responsible buildings with globally recognized certifications.

With expertise spanning LEED, IGBC, GRIHA, and ECBC compliance, we provide comprehensive end-to-end solutions that balance sustainability with practicality and cost-effectiveness.

Our team brings together deep technical knowledge and hands-on experience in green building design, energy modeling, and sustainability strategy to deliver exceptional results for every project.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To enable sustainable and smart built environments across India and beyond. We envision a future where every building contributes positively to the environment, community health, and economic prosperity.

Our Mission

To deliver practical, innovative green building solutions that make sustainability accessible and achievable. We are committed to helping our clients navigate the complexities of green certification while maximizing environmental and financial benefits.

Leadership

Meet Our Founder

Mohammed Shahed Ali

Founder & Principal Consultant

Mohammed Shahed Ali is a seasoned green building professional with extensive experience in sustainability consulting and certification management. His deep understanding of multiple green building rating systems — including LEED, IGBC, GRIHA, and ECBC — has helped numerous projects achieve their sustainability goals.

With a passion for sustainable development and a commitment to practical solutions, Shahed founded EMYR Green Building Consultants to bridge the gap between ambitious sustainability targets and real-world implementation. His expertise spans energy efficiency, sustainable materials, ESG integration, and regulatory compliance.

Under his leadership, EMYR has grown into a trusted name in the green building consultancy space, known for delivering results-driven solutions that create lasting environmental impact.
