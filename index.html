Certifications

Certifications We Handle

Globally recognized green building rating systems for every project type

LEED

Leadership in Energy & Environmental Design

The world's most widely used green building rating system, applicable to all building types globally.

IGBC

Indian Green Building Council

India-focused rating systems for new and existing buildings, factories, townships and more.

GRIHA

Green Rating for Integrated Habitat Assessment

National green building standard endorsed by the Government of India for sustainable habitats.

ECBC

Energy Conservation Building Code

Bureau of Energy Efficiency's mandatory code for energy-efficient commercial buildings in India.
